Nursing Students + Expectations

PAA is proud to offer volunteer experiences that count toward CBL hours. Ask your professor about signing up through the department portal.

Typical volunteers commit to 2–3 hours/month. We also offer flexible one-time event roles.
All volunteers receive orientation training and CPR/First Aid certification opportunities.
Nursing and pre-med students can request hands-on clinical placements supervised by licensed professionals.

Need your hours documented? We provide detailed verification of service hours and responsibilities for your program requirements.
