Our Journey

Key milestones and events that have shaped our organization

January 2024

Hospital Buddy Program Launch

We officially launched our Hospital Buddy Program in partnership with Children's Memorial Hospital. After months of planning and coordination, our first cohort of 12 volunteers began providing companionship and support to pediatric patients.

The program was inspired by feedback from families who expressed the need for additional emotional support during extended hospital stays.

Read More
December 2023

Holiday Gift Drive Success

Our annual Holiday Gift Drive exceeded expectations with over 200 gifts collected for children in the pediatric ward. This was our largest gift drive to date, with participation from multiple campus organizations and local businesses.

The event culminated in a special delivery day where volunteers dressed as holiday characters distributed gifts and organized activities for patients and their families.

Read More
November 2023

Community Health Fair

We organized our first Community Health Fair, bringing together healthcare providers, social service agencies, and families for a day of education and resources. More than 150 community members attended, and 25 organizations provided information and services.

The event featured health screenings, interactive workshops, and a resource fair designed to connect families with pediatric health services.

Read More
October 2023

Pediatric Advocacy Workshop

We hosted our inaugural Pediatric Advocacy Workshop featuring guest speakers from healthcare policy, social work, and family advocacy organizations. The workshop provided training on effective advocacy strategies and healthcare navigation for over 40 student participants.

This event laid the foundation for our ongoing advocacy training program and established important connections with experienced advocates in our community.

Read More
September 2023

PAA Official Launch

Pediatric Advocacy Alliance was officially recognized as a student organization after a successful petition and presentation to the university administration. Our founding members established our mission, vision, and core values during this pivotal moment.

The launch event included a ceremony attended by faculty advisors, student leaders, and representatives from the Children's Memorial Hospital who expressed support for our mission.
