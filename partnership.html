River Forest Community Hospital, Spring 2025

River Forest Community Hospital

Our partnership with River Forest Community Hospital has enabled us to expand our infant wellness program to reach over 300 families in the past year alone.

Through collaborative workshops, donation drives, and their generous support, we've been able to provide essential resources and education to new parents.

Working with PAA has amplified our community impact and brought joy to both our staff and patients.

- Dr. Sarah Johnson, Pediatric Director
