Partnering for Pediatric Advocacy

Thank you to the incredible organizations who help us advocate, uplift, and serve.

Why Partner With Us?

PAA partners with schools, clinics, libraries, and nonprofits to uplift child and family well-being through community-centered service and education.

Our student-led initiatives bring fresh energy, passionate engagement, and meaningful connections to every partnership. Together, we create sustainable impact that benefits both the communities we serve and the future healthcare professionals in our organization.

"PAA brought energy, passion, and meaningful results to our mission. Their student volunteers connect beautifully with our families and bring fresh perspectives to our programs."

— Local Partner
