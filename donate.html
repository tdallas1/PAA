What Your Gift Supports

Your generosity directly impacts children and families in our community. Every dollar makes a difference in these key areas:

Essentials for New Mothers

Provides diapers, formula, and care packages for new mothers and infants in need.

Pediatric Safety Items

Delivers safety equipment, car seats, and childproofing supplies to families with limited resources.

Advocacy Campaigns

Funds educational materials and outreach efforts to promote children's health and welfare policies.

Educational Resources

Creates accessible learning materials for families on child development and health topics.

Event Accessibility

Provides transportation, childcare, translation, and food at community events and workshops.
