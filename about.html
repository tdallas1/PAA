Why We Exist

Pediatric Advocacy Alliance was founded on a simple but urgent truth: too many children are left out of conversations about health, safety, and dignity. We are a student-driven movement working to change that — through service, education, advocacy, and action.

We believe advocacy belongs to everyone. We believe kids deserve systems that protect them, not fail them. And we believe change happens when people show up.

The path we've traveled together so far

Fall 2024
Official Founding
Launched at Dominican University, founded by Trinity Dallas with student advocates passionate about pediatric health. Guided by faculty mentors Dr. William Zic and Dr. James.
🏆
Fall 2024
Building Identity
Growing to 30+ members from diverse academic backgrounds including nursing, social work, business, and biology. Creating early partnerships across campus.
📈
Winter 2025
Turning Point
Restructured our executive board and formalized systems for volunteer tracking and programming. Expanded our reach with new partnerships and campus recognition.
📣
Winter 2025
Advocacy Training
Launched our first advocacy training sessions, equipping members with knowledge in pediatric justice, health policy, and service-based leadership.
🌈
Winter 2025
Academic Integration
Partnered with Dominican's Community-Based Learning program, becoming a designated volunteer site for nursing students completing civic engagement coursework.
🌎
