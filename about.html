Why We Exist

Pediatric Advocacy Alliance was founded on a simple but urgent truth: too many children are left out of conversations about health, safety, and dignity.

We are a student-driven movement working to change that — through service, education, advocacy, and action.

We believe advocacy belongs to everyone. We believe kids deserve systems that protect them, not fail them. And we believe change happens when people show up.

Our Mission:

To advance the health, rights, and well-being of children through service, advocacy, education, and systemic action.

Advocating from the heart, 2024.
