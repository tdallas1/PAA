Programs That Make a Difference

From classrooms to clinics, we bring change where kids and families need it most.

Est. Spring 2025

Bundles of Joy

We create care packages for new and expecting mothers, filled with essentials for both baby and mom. From diapers and onesies to self-care items, each bundle is packed with love and practical support for those transitioning into parenthood.

Learn More
Seasonal Favorite

PAA Reads

Our literacy initiative brings PAA members into local schools and libraries to read with children ages 5-10. We also organize book donation drives and create reading corner setups in underserved communities, fostering a lifelong love of reading.

Get Involved
Core Program

Community Health Outreach

We partner with local healthcare providers to offer health screenings, vaccination clinics, and educational workshops in communities with limited healthcare access. Our campus nursing students get real-world experience while serving families in need.

Sign Up
Annual Flagship

Glow Up for Good

Our signature event transforms a campus space into a spa day for underserved youth. Participants enjoy nail art, face masks, hairstyling, and confidence-building workshops. It's a day of pampering and self-care that reminds every child they deserve to feel special.

Learn More
Leadership Builder

Policy & Advocacy

PAA members engage in legislative advocacy for children's health and welfare. We organize Capitol visits, letter-writing campaigns, and community forums. Students develop leadership skills while fighting for systemic change that impacts children's lives.

Get Involved
Partnership Program

Education Partners

We collaborate with K-12 schools to provide after-school tutoring, college prep workshops, and career day presentations. PAA members mentor students, share healthcare career paths, and help bridge educational gaps in underserved communities.
