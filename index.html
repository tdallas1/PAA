Our Story

Cultivating compassion and action through pediatric advocacy

Who We Are

Pediatric Advocacy Alliance (PAA) is an interdisciplinary, student-led organization dedicated to advancing the health, rights, and well-being of children especially in underserved communities.

Learn More

What We Do

Through hands-on service projects, educational initiatives, and community outreach, we create meaningful connections between students, healthcare professionals, and the families we serve.

Our Projects

Our Mission

We strive to create a more equitable healthcare system where every child receives the support, dignity, and care they deserve by empowering students to become compassionate advocates.
