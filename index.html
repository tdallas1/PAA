About PAA

Who We Are

The Pediatric Advocacy Alliance (PAA) is a student-led organization dedicated to supporting children's health and welfare initiatives on campus and in our community.

What We Do

Through direct service, fundraising, and awareness campaigns, we work to improve the lives of children facing medical challenges and their families.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves access to quality healthcare, emotional support, and the opportunity to thrive. Our mission is to advocate for children's rights and needs while providing meaningful experiences for our members to grow as compassionate leaders.
