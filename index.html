About PAA

Our Story

The Pediatric Advocacy Alliance (PAA) was founded at Dominican University with a mission to advance the health, rights, and well-being of children—especially those in underserved communities—through advocacy, education, and outreach.

Founded by Trinity, PAA was born from a personal commitment to pediatric health, equity, and community-based care. We believe that every child deserves access to quality healthcare, education, and support regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our dedicated team of student advocates works tirelessly to create meaningful change in our community and beyond, partnering with healthcare professionals, community organizations, and policymakers to amplify our impact.

Our Core Pillars

Advocacy

We advocate for policies and initiatives that promote children's health and well-being through legislative action, campaigns, and awareness events. Our advocacy efforts focus on creating sustainable change at local, state, and national levels.

Education

We provide educational resources and programs including workshops, panels, first aid training, and health literacy programs. Our goal is to empower communities with knowledge and skills to support children's health needs.

Outreach

We engage directly with communities through volunteering, donation drives, and collaborations with outside organizations. Our outreach initiatives aim to provide immediate support while building lasting relationships with the communities we serve.
