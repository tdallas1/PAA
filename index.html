Who We Are

The Pediatric Advocacy Alliance is a student-led organization dedicated to improving children's health and wellbeing through education, advocacy, and service. Founded by healthcare students, we work to address the unique needs of children in our communities.

Learn More

What We Do

We organize community health initiatives, educational workshops, donation drives, and volunteer opportunities that directly impact children's lives. Our programs are designed to address healthcare disparities and promote health equity for all children.

Learn More

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to become effective advocates for children's health and wellbeing. We believe that by combining education, advocacy, and direct service, we can create lasting positive change in pediatric healthcare and family support systems.
