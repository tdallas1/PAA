Welcome to Pediatric Advocacy Alliance

At the heart of our work is a belief in dignity, equity, and care—for every child, every family, and every future. Through purpose-driven programming and collaborative action, we empower students to lead with compassion, grow through service, and champion change where it matters most.

Monthly Observance

Child Abuse Prevention Month - April 2025

April is National Child Abuse Prevention Month, a time to recognize the importance of families and communities working together to prevent child abuse and neglect. At PAA, we're dedicated to promoting awareness and providing resources to help build safe, stable, and nurturing relationships for all children.
