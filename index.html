Our Story

Cultivating compassion and action through pediatric advocacy

Who We Are

Pediatric Advocacy Alliance (PAA) is an interdisciplinary, student-led organization dedicated to advancing the health, rights, and well-being of children especially in underserved communities. We bring together student advocates, future healthcare leaders, educators, social workers, and changemakers to raise awareness, promote equity, and build supportive systems for children and families through service, education, and advocacy.

Read More

What We Do

Through hands-on service projects, educational initiatives, and community outreach, we create meaningful connections between students, healthcare professionals, and the families we serve. Our programs address the unique challenges faced by pediatric patients while providing valuable experiential learning for our members.

Get Involved

Our Mission

We strive to create a more equitable healthcare system where every child receives the support, dignity, and care they deserve. By empowering students to become compassionate advocates, we're building a future healthcare community that prioritizes holistic, family-centered care and addresses systemic barriers to health equity.
